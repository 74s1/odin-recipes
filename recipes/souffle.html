Souffle Recipe

Crispy on the outside and soft and fluffy on the inside, a soufflé can be filled with many of your favourite ingredients. Making a soufflé is actually a simple process, despite its reputation for being difficult to make. Try adding cheese, crab, vegetables and more for a decadent dinner.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • flour
  • salt
  • pinch pepper
  • milk
  • egg yolks
  • egg whites
  • cream of tartar

Steps

  1. preheat oven
  2. melt butter, stir in flour, salt and pepper.
  3. cook stirring constantly
  4. stir in milk
  5. stir until boil
  6. seperate eggs
  7. combine yolk mixture
  8. beat egg whites
  9. pour into 4 cup souffle
  10. bake
